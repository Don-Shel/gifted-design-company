    The Beginning

    Gifted Company was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate entrepreneurs who believed that everyone has a unique gift to offer. Our founders came from diverse backgrounds, but shared a common vision of creating a platform that would help people discover and develop their talents.

    Milestones

    • 2016

      We launched our first online course, "Unlocking Your Potential," which helped over 1,000 people discover their strengths and weaknesses.

    • 2017

      We expanded our services to include one-on-one coaching and mentorship, helping hundreds of individuals achieve their career goals.

    • 2018

      We launched our mobile app, allowing users to access our resources and community on-the-go.

    Our Mission

    At Gifted Company, our mission is to help people discover and develop their unique gifts and talents, and connect them with opportunities that will take their careers to the next level. We believe that everyone has the potential to make a meaningful impact, and we're committed to helping them achieve their goals.
